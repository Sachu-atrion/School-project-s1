About

_________________

GHS MAMALAKANDAM was established in 1983 and it is managed by the Department of Education. It is located in Rural area. It is located in KOTHAMANGALAM block of ERNAKULAM district of Kerala. The school consists of Grades from 5 to 10. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is Not Applicable in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Malayalam is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.
